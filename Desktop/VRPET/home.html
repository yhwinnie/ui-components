About

Explore with your virtual reality pet

Virtual Reality Pet is a Google Cardboard virtual reality experience built to relieve stress and anxiety. Enjoy a handcrafted world built for you and your pet.

Play fetch with your pet by finding a stick and picking it up.

Fish by walking up to a body of water and casting your fishing rod.

Create a campfire by cutting down a tree and lighting the logs that are left behind.
